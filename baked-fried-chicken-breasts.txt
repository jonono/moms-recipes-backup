Baked Fried Chicken Breasts

INGREDIENTS

1 tsp. minced fresh thyme
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. paprika
1/2 tsp. dry mustard
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 1/2 cups buttermilk
4 bone in skin-on chicken breasts, skin removed
1 1/2 cups panko bread crumbs
olive oil

DIRECTIONS

1. In small bowl, stir thyme, garlic powder, paprika, mustard, salt and pepper and set aside.  In separate bowl, whisk buttermilk and half the thyme mixture.  Add chicken and turn to coat.  Cover and refrigerate for 8 hours or up to 24 hours.  
2. In shallow dish, combine panko and remaining thyme mixture.  Remove chicken from marinade, discarding marinade.  Press chicken into panko mixture to coat.  
3. Place on greased baking sheet.  Spray with oil an bake in 375 oven, about 30 minutes.  Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
