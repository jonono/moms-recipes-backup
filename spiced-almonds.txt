Spiced Almonds

unusual flavour of fennel

INGREDIENTS

1/4 cup sugar
1 1/2 tsp crushed fennel seeds
1 tsp. red pepper flakes
1 tsp. coarse sea salt
2 cups natural almonds
1 tbsp water

DIRECTIONS

1. Line baking sheet with parchment paper.  Combine first 4 ingredients in bowl.  Add almonds and water.  Stir to combine.  Spread on pan and bake at 325 for 25-30 minutes, stirring twice.  Cool and store in airtight container for up to a week or freeze for up to a month.
