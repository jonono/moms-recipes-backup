Pie Chart

INGREDIENTS

Apple - 8 cups thinly sliced apples, 1/2 cup brown sugar, 3 Tbsp. flour, 2 Tbsp. lemon juice, 1/2 tsp. cinnamon
Blueberry - 5 cups berries, 2/3 cup sugar, 3 Tbsp. cornstarch, 1 tsp. grated lemon rind
Peach or Nectarine - 6 cups sliced, 3/4 cup sugar, 2 Tbsp. cornstarch, 2 Tbsp. lemon juice, 1/4 tsp. ginger and/or nutmeg
Rhubarb - 5 cups chopped, 1 1/4 cups sugar, 1/4 cup cornstarch, pinch salt
Saskatoons - 4 cups, 3/4 cup sugar, 2 Tbsp. flour, 2 Tbsp. lemon juice

DIRECTIONS

1. Bake on lower rack at 425 for 15 minutes.  Increase heat to 350 and bake about another 40 minutes.
