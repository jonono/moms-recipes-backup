Orange and Black Pepper Strawberries

INGREDIENTS

12 large fresh strawberries
125 ml. orange juice
50 ml. Grand Marnier
1/4 tsp. black pepper
sugar to taste
1/2 oz. butter

DIRECTIONS

1. Add butter in pan and saute strawberries 1 minute.  Add Grand Marnier.  Remove strawberries and keep warm.  Add orange juice, pepper and sugar to taste.  Reduce liquid by half and return strawberries to pan.  Serve with good vanilla ice cream.
