Lemon and Herb Rubbed Pork Tenderloin

INGREDIENTS

2 garlic cloves, minced
1 Tbsp. lemon zest
4 sprigs fresh parsley, finely chopped
2 sprigs fresh thyme, finely chopped
1 sprig fresh rosemary, finely chopped
2 Tbsp. canola oil
1/2 tsp. sea salt
1/2 tsp. chili pepper flakes
1 pork tenderloin , well trimmed about 12 oz.

DIRECTIONS

1. Combine rub mixture and rub all over pork.  Place in zip lock bag and refrigerate 24 hours.   Preheat oven to 400.  Arrange pork on foil lined, rimmed baking sheet and roast in centre of oven until thermometer registers 155 degrees, 20 to 25 minutes.  Cover loosely with foil and let rest 5 minutes before slicing thickly.
