Chocolate Cake

INGREDIENTS

2 cups flour
2/3 cup cocoa
1 1/4 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. baking powder
3 eggs
1 2/3 cups sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
1 cup mayo
1 1/3 cup water

DIRECTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Combine dry ingredients, mix well and set aside.
2. Beat together eggs, sugar and vanilla in large bowl for 3 minutes, using high speed.  Reduce speed to low and beat in may until blended.
3. Add flour mixture in batches alternating with water in 4 equal additions.  Pour batter into cake pans.
4. Bake 30 to 35 minutes until center of cake springs back or when toothpick comes out clean.  Cool in pans for 10 minutes, then remove and allow to cool completely before frosting.
