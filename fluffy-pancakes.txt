Fluffy Pancakes

INGREDIENTS

2 cups flour
4 tbsp. sugar
2 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
2 eggs 
2 cups buttermilk
4 tbsp. butter, melted

DIRECTIONS

1. Combine dry ingredients in a bowl.  In another bowl, whisk eggs and butter into buttermilk.  Let sit about 10 minutes.  Add to dry ingredients and whisk until no lumps are left.  Add fruit of your choice such as chopped banana, berries...etc.
