Warm Brie and Pear Salad with Raspberry Dressing

INGREDIENTS

1 bag spinach leaves
1/2 lb. Brie
2 ripe pears, seeded and thinly sliced
3 Tbsp. raspberry vinegar
1/3 cup olive oil
1 Tbsp. sugar
1/4 cup raspberries, mashed

DIRECTIONS

1. Whisk together dressing ingredients.  Divide lettuce onto salad plates.  Wrap Brie cheese and soften in microwave at med for 45 seconds.  Slice cheese carefully and place on salad.  Arrange pear slices and add dressing.  Garnish with whole raspberries and serve immediately.
