Blueberry Cornmeal Cobbler

INGREDIENTS

7 cups fresh blueberries
1/3 cup sugar
1 tbsp. cornstarch
1/2 tsp. almond extract
Biscuit topping
1 cup flour
1/3 cup cornmeal
1/4 cup sugar
1 tsp. each baking powder and ground ginger
1/4 tsp. baking soda
pinch salt
1/4 cold butter, cubed
3/4 cup buttermilk

DIRECTIONS

1. Gently toss berries with sugar, cornstarch and almond extract.  Scrape into 8" square baking dish.
2. In bowl, whisk together flour, 1/4 cup of the cornmeal, sugar, baking powder, ginger, baking soda and salt.  Using pastry blender, cut in butter until mixture resembles crumbs.  Drizzle in buttermilk, stirring with fork to form soft, sticky dough.  With spoon, drop 9 evenly spaced mounds over blueberry mixture.  Sprinkle with remaining cornmeal.  Bake in 400 oven about 35 minutes until topping is light golden and no longer doughy.  Serve warm with ice cream.
