Garlic Roasted Potatoes

INGREDIENTS

2-3 pounds small red or white potatoes
1/3 cup olive oil
1 1/2 tsp. kosher salt
1 tsp. ground pepper
6 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 tsp. dried oregano
1/4 tsp. dried thyme

DIRECTIONS

1. Blend all together.  Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Place on parchment lined pan and bake 45 minutes.  When cooked, toss with some fresh parsley in serving bowl.
