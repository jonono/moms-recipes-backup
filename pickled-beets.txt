Pickled Beets

INGREDIENTS

10 pounds fresh small beets, stems removed
2 cups white sugar
1 Tbsp. picking salt
1 quart vinegar
1/4 cup whole cloves

DIRECTIONS

1. Cover beets in a large pot with water and bring boil.  Cook until tender, about 15 minutes.  If beets are large, cut them into quarters.  Drain, reserving 2 cups beet water.  Cool beets and peel.
2. In sterilized jars, add several whole cloves.
3. In large saucepan, combine sugar, beet water, vinegar and pickling salt.  Bring to rapid boil and pour over beets in jars.  Seal lids.
4. Refrigerate jars until ready to eat.
