Blueberry Cornmeal Muffins

INGREDIENTS

1 cup flour
1/2 cup + 1 Tbsp. cornmeal
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda
2/3 cup sugar
pinch salt
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup buttermilk
1 egg
3/4 cup blueberries

DIRECTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees and line muffin cups.  In large bowl, mix dry ingredients.  In measuring cup, pour oil and buttermilk and whisk in the egg.  Stir the egg mixture into dry ingredients and fold in half blueberries.  Divide into muffin cups and drop remaining blueberries on top.  Bake in oven 15 to 20 minutes.
