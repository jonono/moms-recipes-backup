Parmesan Pesto Salad

INGREDIENTS

1 cup Miracle Whip salad dressing
1/2 cup finely chopped basil
1/4 grated Parmesan cheese
2 Tbsp. milk
2 cloves garlic, minced
3 cups cooked penne pasta
1 cup each julienne carrots and zucchini
1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved

DIRECTIONS

1. Combine salad dressing, basil, parmesan cheese, milk and garlic.  Mix well.
2. Add all remaining ingredients. Mix lightly.  Chill
3. Sprinkle with additional parmesan cheese.
