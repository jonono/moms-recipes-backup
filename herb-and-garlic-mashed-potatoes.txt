Herb and Garlic Mashed Potatoes

Easy and delicious

Yield: About 8 servings

INGREDIENTS

8 cups (2L0 cubed peeled russet potatoes
3 cloves garlic, peeled
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup lite sour cream
1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
2 tbsp. chopped fresh parsley
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. thyme, crumbled
1/4 tsp. oregano, crumbled
1/4 tsp. rosemary, crumbled

DIRECTIONS

1. Cook potatoes and garlic in boiling salted water until tender, then drain.  Add remaining ingredients and mash with a potato masher until smooth.
