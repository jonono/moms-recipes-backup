Classic Baked Beans

INGREDIENTS

1 onion, finely chopped
4 slices bacon, chopped
1/2 cup fancy molasses
1/2 cup ketchup
1/4 cup Dijon mustard
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. chili flakes
2 cups dry navy beans, rinsed
341-ml bottle regular beer

DIRECTIONS

1. Stir onion with bacon, molasses, ketchup, Dijon, salt and chili flakes in slow cooker.  Stir in beans, beer and 3 cups water until combined.  Cover and cook on low, about 10 hours.
