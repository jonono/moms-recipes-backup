Best Ever Banana Bread

INGREDIENTS

2 eggs, beaten
1/3 cup buttermilk
1/2 cup oil
1 cup mashed bananas
1 1/2 cups white sugar
1 3/4/ cups flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 cup chopped pecans (optional)

DIRECTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees.  Spray a loaf pan.  Blend eggs, buttermilk, oil and bananas.  Sift dry ingredients and add to egg mixture.  Stir in pecans and mix well.  Pour into loaf pan and bake 1 hour, 20 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.
