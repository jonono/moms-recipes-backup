Mango Black Bean Salsa

INGREDIENTS

1 large mango, peeled, pitted and finely diced
1 avocado, finely diced
1 - 15oz can black beans (no salt), rinsed and drained
1/3 cup diced red onion
1 fresh jalapeno pepper, seeded and diced
1/2 cup cilantro, chopped
juice of 1/2 lime
1/2 tsp. fine sea salt

DIRECTIONS

1. Add all in a bowl and stir gently to combine.  Cover and refrigerate up to a day to blend flavours  Taste before serving and add more salt or lime juice if desired.
