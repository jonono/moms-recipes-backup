Asparagus Salad with Four-Flavour Dressing

Serves about 6-8

INGREDIENTS

1 3/4 lbs. asparagus, stems trimmed
1/4 cup red pepper, finely chopped
3 Tbsp. soy sauce
1 Tbsp. oil
1 Tbsp. rice vinegar
2 tsp. honey
1/2 tsp. sesame oil
1 tsp. Asian style hot chili sauce
1 med. garlic clove minced
1 tsp. finely chopped ginger

DIRECTIONS

1. Boil asparagus about 90 seconds.  Drain well. Cool in ice cold water and drain well again.  Arrange on a serving platter.  Cover and refrigerate until needed.
2. Combine remaining ingredients in a bowl.  Cover and refrigerate until needed as well.
3. When ready to serve, uncover asparagus and drizzle with dressing.  Let the asparagus meld with the dressing about 20 minutes before serving.
