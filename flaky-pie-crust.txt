Flaky Pie Crust

the best pie crust recipe that works for me

INGREDIENTS

3 cups flour
1 tsp. salt
1/2 cup cold unsalted butter, cubed
1/2 cup cold lard, cubed
1 egg
2 tsp. vinegar
ice water

DIRECTIONS

1. Whisk flour and salt.  Cut in butter and lard until coarse crumbs.  Beat egg with vinegar and add water to make 2/3 cup.  Drizzle over flour.  Divide in half and press into discs.  Wrap and refrigerate 30 minutes or up to 2 days.
