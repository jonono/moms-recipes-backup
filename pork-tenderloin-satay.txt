Pork Tenderloin Satay

INGREDIENTS

2 cloves garlic, minced
1/3 cup fresh cilantro 
1/4 cup honey
1/4 cup peanut butter
2 Tbsp. hoisin sauce
1 Tbsp. soy sauce
2 Tbsp. lime juice
1 Tbsp. frozen orange juice concentrate
1 Tbsp chopped ginger root
1/2 tsp. Tabasco
1 tsp. sesame oil
3 lb pork tenderloin, trimmed
1/2 cup chicken stock
1/2 cup  orange juice

DIRECTIONS

1. In large bowl, mix together first ingredients (to sesame oil) until smooth.  Add pork and turn to coat well with marinade.  Cover overnight or for 2 to 3 hours.
2. Remove pork, reserving marinade, place on foil lined baking sheet and bake at 350 for 45-60 minutes.  
3. Meanwhile, in small saucepan combine reserved marinade with chicken stock and orange juice. Cook over med heat, stirring constantly for about 5 minutes or until mixture boils and thickens slightly.  Drizzle over slice pork.
