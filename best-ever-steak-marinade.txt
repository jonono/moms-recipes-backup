Best Ever Steak Marinade

INGREDIENTS

2 med shallots, minced
1 Tbsp. dried thyme leaves
3 Tbsp. brown sugar
1/4 cup soy sauce
3 Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
2 Tbsp. balsamic vinegar
1/3 cup oil

DIRECTIONS

1. Combine all and place in heavy duty zip top bag or plastic container.  Add steaks and seal.  Place in fridge overnight.  If using a bag, turn bag over often.
