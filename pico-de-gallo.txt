Pico de Gallo

classic basic salsa

INGREDIENTS

1 large ripe tomato or a couple of regular sized ones
1/2 red onion
1 jalapeno pepper
fresh cilantro
juice of a 1/2 lime
salt

DIRECTIONS

1. Chop tomatoes in small pieces.  In small chopper, chop onion and jalapeno together and add to tomato along with chopped cilantro, lime juice and sea salt.  Let flavors blend for a few minutes.
