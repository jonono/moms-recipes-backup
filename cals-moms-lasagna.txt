Cal's Mom's Lasagna

INGREDIENTS

1 1/2 lbs. ground beef
1 clove garlic, minced
1 Tbsp. fresh parsley flakes
1 Tbsp. fresh basil
1/2 tsp. salt
28 oz. can of diced tomatoes (not drained)
2 - 6 oz cans tomato paste
6 Lasagna noodles
3 cups or 750g cream style cottage cheese (regular 2% MF)
2 beaten eggs
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
2 tbsp. parsley flakes
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 lb (or bag) of mozzarella cheese shredded

DIRECTIONS

1. Brown meat slowly and spoon off excess fat.  Add next 6 ingredients.  Simmer uncovered 30 minutes to blend flavours, stirring occasionally.
2. Cook noodle in boiling salted water until tender; drain; rinse in cold water.  Meanwhile, combine cottage cheese with eggs, seasonings and Parmesan cheese.
3. Place half the noodles in 13x9x2" baking dish; spread half the cottage cheese mixture over and add half the mozzarella cheese and  half the meat mixture.  Repeat layers and cover top layr with mozza cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees 30 to 40 minutes.  Let stand 10-15 minutes (important) before cutting.
