Baked Salmon

INGREDIENTS

2 cups brown rice
4 cups water
2 lb. fresh Atlantic salmon fillet
1 1/2 cup orange juice
1 1/2 tsp. dried rosemary
1 1/2 tsp. dried dill weed
1 1/2 tsp. dried basil
1 1/2 tsp. lemon pepper
1 1/2 tsp. dry mustard

DIRECTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  In a saucepan, bring 3 1/2 cups water to boil.  Add rice and stir.  Reduce heat, cover and simmer 20 minutes.
2. In large pan, add just enough water to cover the bottom of the pan.  Lay salmon fillet in the pan, skin side down.  Place cooked rice around the outside of the salmon.  Sprinkle the orange juice over fish and rice.
3. In a small bowl, combine herbs and sprinkle over fish and rice too.
4. Cover with foil and bake in preheated over for 30 to 40 minutes or until fish is tender and flaky.
