Orzo Risotto with Peas & Mint

INGREDIENTS

2 tsp. olive oil
3/4 cup orzo
1 cup vegetable broth
1 cup water
1/2 cup frozen peas
2 Tbsp. chopped fresh mint

DIRECTIONS

1. Heat a med saucepan over med heat.  Add oil, then orzo.  Stir until orzo is coated.  Pour in broth and water.  Boil, then reduce heat to medium.  Stir occasionally, until orzo is tender, 7 to 8 minutes.  Add peas and stir until warmed through, 2 to 3 minutes.  Cover to keep warm.  Orzo will absorb liquid as it sits.  Stir in mint and serve immediately.

NOTES

Serves 4
