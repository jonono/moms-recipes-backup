Deep Dish Turkey Pie

INGREDIENTS

2 Tbsp. butter
1/4 cup chopped celery
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 cup milk (or leftover gravy)
1/2 tsp. poultry seasoning
1/4 tsp. pepper
4  cups cooked turkey (cubed)
1 pkg mixed vegetables (or use your own)
1/2 pkg Pillsbury refrigerated pie crusts, softened as directed (1 crust)

DIRECTIONS

1. Heat oven to 400 degrees.  In Dutch oven, melt butter and add celery and onion.  Cook until tender and stir in soup, milk, poultry seasoning and pepper.  Cook until heated, then add turkey and vegetables.  Pour into ungreased 2 quart casserole.  Remove pie crust from pouch; place flat over turkey mixture.  Roll up edge of crust to fit top of dish.  Flute edges.  Cut slits in several places.  Bake 40 to 50 minutes or until golden brown.  Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
