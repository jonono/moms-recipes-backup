Rocky Pointe Shrimp Cocktail

INGREDIENTS

1 lb. shrimp, peeled, deveined, cooked and chopped into small pieces
1 large tomato, chopped
1 cucumber, peeled and chopped
1 cup chunky salsa
1/4 cup fresh lime juice
2 tbsp. bottled hot pepper sauce
1/2 cup rough chopped cilantro
1 avocado, chopped (optional)
tortilla chips (like using scoops)

DIRECTIONS

1. In large bowl, fold together all  ingredients except chips.  Chill 20 minutes.  Stir again and chill.  Serve with tortilla chips.
