Glacial Refresher

a flavoured water recipe

INGREDIENTS

1/2 English cucumber
5 cups ice cubes
10 fresh or frozen cranberries , halved
4 thin lemon slices
4 thin orange slices
4 thin slices peeled fresh ginger
4 sprigs fresh parsley
5 cups cold water

DIRECTIONS

1. Cut 4 thin slices lengthwise from cucumber half;  Place a layer of ice cubes in bottom of large clear glass pitcher.  Add cucumber with next 5 ingredients.  Top with remaining ice cubes.  Pour cold water over top and refrigerate 1 hour.
