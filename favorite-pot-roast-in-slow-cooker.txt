Favorite Pot Roast in Slow Cooker

INGREDIENTS

3 to 4lb. beef rump or chuck roast
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. seasoned salt
1/4 tsp. seasoned pepper
1/4 tsp. paprika
1 Tbsp. instant minced onion
1 cup beef bouillon

DIRECTIONS

1. Rub all sides of beef with salt, seasoned salt, seasoned pepper and paprika.  Combine seasoned beef with onion and bouillon in slow cooker.  Cover and cook low 8 to 10 hours.  Remove from pot and slice.  If desired, vegetables can be added to pot along with beef such as potatoes, small white onions, carrots and turnips.  Gravy can be made after removal of meat by dissolving some flour in cold water and adding to juice for about 30 minutes on high.
