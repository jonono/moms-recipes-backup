Dijon Cheddar Crostini

INGREDIENTS

3 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 cup Dijon mustard
1/4 cup mayonnaise
2 tbps. finely chopped green onion
6 dozen baguette slices 3/8" thick

DIRECTIONS

1. Combine all ingredients except baguette slices.  Spread mixture evenly over one side of each baguette slice.  Place on jelly roll pans.  May be prepared to this point and frozen for up to a month.  Bake at 350 for 7 to 10 minutes or until cheese is melted and crostinis are heated through.
