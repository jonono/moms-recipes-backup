Merry Cranberry Muffins

INGREDIENTS

2 cups fresh cranberries
3/4 cup Miracle Whip dressing
1/4 cup orange juice concentrate, thawed, undiluted
2 eggs, beaten
2 cups flour
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup chopped nuts
2 tsp. grated orange rind
1 tsp. each baking powder and baking soda

DIRECTIONS

1. Heat oven to 350 degrees.  Combine cranberries, salad dressing, orange juice and eggs.  In separate bowl, combine remaining ingredients.  Stir wet mixture into dry ingredients.  Fill muffin cups and bake 20 to 25 minutes
