Sante Fe Chicken

INGREDIENTS

1 1/2 cups instant white rice, uncooked
1 can (19 oz) black beans, rinsed
1 green or yellow pepper
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 soup can water
chopped fresh cilantro
4 small boneless chicken breasts, cut up in large chunks (about 1 lb)
1 cup salsa
1 cup tex mex shredded cheese

DIRECTIONS

1. Heat oven to 400.
2. Combine rice, beans and peppers in 13x9 inch baking dish.  Mix soup, water, then pour over rice mixture. Top with chicken pieces and salsa.  Sprinkle cilantro over top.  Bake 35 minutes or until chicken is done.  Top with cheese and bake 10 minutes until melted.
