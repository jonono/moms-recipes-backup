Hoisin Chicken

INGREDIENTS

1/2 cup  hoisin sauce
2 Tbsp. balsamic vinegar
1/4 tsp. pepper
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts

DIRECTIONS

1. Combine hoisin sauce, vinegar and pepper in heavy ziploc bad.  Add chicken and squeeze to coat meat.  Seal and let stand about an hour.  Discard marinade and grill over med heat.
