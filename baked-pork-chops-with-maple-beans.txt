Baked Pork Chops with Maple Beans

INGREDIENTS

1 lb. pork chops (3 to 4 butterfly chops are best)
2 tsp. oil
1 tsp. minced garlic
2 - 14 oz can pork and beans in tomato sauce
2 Tbsp. Dijon mustard
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup maple syrup
1/2 cup BBQ sauce
1 tsp. fresh rosemary, chopped
salt and pepper

DIRECTIONS

1. Lightly salt and pepper the chops on both sides and fry in oil and garlic just to sear.  Empty cans of beans in large greased baking dish.  Place chops over top beans.  In small bowl, blend mustard, brown sugar, maple syrup, barbecue sauce and rosemary.  Pour over chops.  Bake uncovered at 325 degrees about 80 minutes.
