Slow Cooker Home-Style Beef

INGREDIENTS

3/4 cup beer
2/3 cup Kraft brown sugar, BBQ sauce, divided
1 Tbsp. Dijon mustard
1 Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
2 onions, sliced
1 boneless roast, about 2 lbs.
2 tsp. cornstarch

DIRECTIONS

1. Mix beer, 1/2 cup barbecue sauce and mustard.  Place onions in slow cooker; top with meat and sauce.  Cover with lid.
2. Cook on low 7 to 8 hours or high 5 to 6 hours.  Remove meat and onions reserving liquid.  Whisk cornstarch and remaining BBQ sauce in small bowl first and then into slow cooker.  Cook covered on high until thickened.  Drizzle meat and onions with sauce.
