Salted Caramel Butter Bars

INGREDIENTS

2 cups butter, softenened
1 cup white sugar
1.5 cups powdered sugar
1 tbsp. vanilla
1 tbsp. coarse sea salt
4 cups flour
1 14oz pkg. caramels, unwrapped
1/3 whipping cream
1/2 tsp. vanilla

DIRECTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Line a 9X13 pan with parchment paper.  Lightly spray with Pam.
2. In a large mixing bowl, cream butte and sugars.  Add vanilla and beat until combined.  Mix in flour and combine until it forms a soft dough.  
3. Press half the dough in bottom of pan and place the other half in fridge.  Bake in oven for 15 minutes.
4. Remove the pan from oven.   Melt caramels, cream and 1/2 tsp vanilla in microwave for 1 minute.  Pour evenly over hot crust and slightly sprinkle with sea salt.  
5. With remaining dough, crumble over top and return to oven for 25-30 minutes until filling is bubbly and top is firm.  Cool completely before cutting.
