Cocoa Banana Bran Muffins

INGREDIENTS

1 1/4 cups All Bran Original cereal
3/4 cup milk
1 cup flour
1/2 cup lightly packed brown sugar
1/4 cup cocoa
1 tbsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. each of baking soda and salt
1 egg
1/4 cup canola oil
3 large, ripe bananas
(reserve 12 thin slices, mash remainder)
1/3 cup chocolate chips

DIRECTIONS

1. Combine cereal and milk in medium bowl and let sit.  In large bowl, combine dry ingredients.   Beat egg, oil and mashed bananas and stir into cereal mixture.  Now stir everything into dry ingredients until just combined.  Fold in choc chips.  Spoon  into muffin tins well greased or paper lined.  Top with reserved banana slices.  Bake in preheated 400 degree oven to a minimum 20 minutes.
