Ginger, Cilantro and Coconut Rice

INGREDIENTS

1 can coconut milk
1 cup basmati rice
1 tsp. freshly grated ginger
1/3 cup packed fresh cilantro
1/3 cup shredded toasted coconut
2 Tbsp. butter
1/4 tsp. salt

DIRECTIONS

1. Bring coconut milk plus enough water to make 3 cups to a simmer in a saucepan.  Add basmati rice and ginger, reduce heat to low and cook, covered for 20 minutes.  Meanwhile, pulse the cilantro and coconut until well blended and chunky.  Fluff rice with fork and stir in coconut cilantro along with butter and salt.  Serves 6.
