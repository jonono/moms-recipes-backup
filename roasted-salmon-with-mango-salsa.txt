Roasted Salmon with Mango Salsa

INGREDIENTS

Salsa
1 small mango, peeled and finely choppped
1/4 cup pineapple, peeled and finely diced (about 1 cup)
1 Tbsp finely chopped red onion
1/2 roasted red pepper, skin, seeds and core removed, finely diced
1 small jalapeno, seeds and ribs removed, finely diced
1 Tbsp. chopped fresh mint
1 Tbsp. chopped fresh cilantro
Salmon
2 lbs. salmon fillets, skin removed
1 Tbsp. olive oil
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 Tbsp. grated lemon peel
1 Tbsp. chopped fresh thyme

DIRECTIONS

1. Combine salsa ingredients and refrigerate.  Arrange salmon on a baking sheet line with parchment paper.  Rub with oil, salt, pepper, lemon peel and thyme.  Roast in preheated oven at 425 for 15 to 18 minutes.  Serve with salsa.
