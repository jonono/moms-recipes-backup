Cocoa Banana Cake

INGREDIENTS

2/3 cup milk
1 Tbsp. vinegar
3/4 cup butter
1 2/3 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 1/4 cup pureed banana
1 tsp. vanilla
2 cups flour
3/4 cup cocoa
1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. soda
3/4 tsp. salt

DIRECTIONS

1. Mix milk and vinegar and set aside.  Cream butter with sugar and eggs.  Stir in banana and vanilla.  Blend dry ingredients and add to cream mixture alternately with milk.  Divide between 2 - 9" round cake pans, greased and floured.  Bake at 350 for 40 to 45 minutes.  Cool 10 minutes in pan before turning out on wire racks.  Cool completely before frosting.  
2. Banana Cocoa Frosting
3. 1/4 cup melted butter
4. 1/2 cup cocoa
5. Combine and blend in 
6. 1/4 cup pureed banana, 2 Tbsp. milk and 3 cups sifted  icing sugar.
