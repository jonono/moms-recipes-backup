Old Fashioned Chewy Ginger Cookies

INGREDIENTS

1 cup sugar
1/2 cup lard, softened (or butter)
2 eggs
1 cup molasses
3 1/2 cups flour
1 Tbsp. ground ginger
1 1/4 tsp. soda
1 tsp. ground cloves
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
additional sugar

DIRECTIONS

1. Cream sugar and lard or butter together until light and fluffy.  Beat in eggs one at a time, then beat in molasses.  
2. In separate bowl, sift flour, ginger, baking soda, cloves and cinnamon.  Add the dry ingredients in 3 batches into creamed mixture, mixing well after each addition.  Knead the dough gently until smooth.  Cover and refrigerate 2 hours.  
3. Grease baking sheet.  Preheat oven to 375.  Form dough into 3 cm. balls and roll in sugar.  Place on baking sheet and bake 10 to 12 minutes.  Cool on rack and store in airtight container.
