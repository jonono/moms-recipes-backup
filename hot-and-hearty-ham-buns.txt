Hot and Hearty Ham Buns

INGREDIENTS

1/4 cup margarine
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. oregano
1/2 cup mayo
2 tsp. Dijon mustard
1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
2 cups cubed ham
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
6 buns, split

DIRECTIONS

1. Combine melted margarine, garlic powder and oregano and brush on cut sides of buns.  Place on baking sheet and broil until golden.
2. In a bowl, combine remaining ingredients.  Spoon about 1/3 cup onto each bun bottom.  Broil for 3-4 minutes to heat through and melt cheese.  Replace bun tops.
