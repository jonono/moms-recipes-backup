Curry Roasted Cauliflower

INGREDIENTS

1 head cauliflower
2 tbsp. olive oil
1/2 tsp. curry powder
1/4 tsp. ground cumin
1/4 tsp. onion powder
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. sea salt
1/2 tsp. black pepper
1 tsp. lemon juice
2 tbsp. fresh cilantro

DIRECTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 475 degrees.
2. Cut the cauliflower florets into 2" pieces.  On a large baking sheet, combine the cauliflower, olive oil, curry powder, cumin, onion powder, garlic powder, salt and pepper.  Toss to coat and spread out in a single layer.  Roast until tender and nicely browned, 15 to 20 minutes.  Remove from the oven, sprinkle with lemon juice and cilantro.  Makes 5 servings.
