Wife Saver

Serves 8

INGREDIENTS

1 loaf crusty bread, thinly sliced
16 slices Cdn back bacon or less of thinly sliced ham
1 - 2 cups grated Cheddar or Gouda
6 large eggs
1/2 tsp. each salt and pepper
1 tsp. grainy mustard
1/4 cup minced or grated onion
1/4 cup finely chopped red pepper
1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
3 cups WHOLE milk
dash Tabasco
Morning
1/3 cup butter
crushed Corn Flakes

DIRECTIONS

1. Layer half the bread in bottom of buttered 9x13 baking dish.  Layer with ham or bacon and scatter cheese on top.  Cover with remaining slices of bread to make it like a sandwich.
2. In med bowl, beat eggs with salt, pepper, mustard, onion, red pepper, Worcestershire sauce, milk and Tabasco.  Pour over sandwiches, cover and let stand in the fridge overnight.
3. In the morning, preheat the oven to 350 degrees.  Melt the butter and pour over the casserole, then cover with roughly crushed cornflakes.  Bake, uncovered, for 45 minutes to an hour and let sit 10 minutes before serving.  
4. Serve with fresh fruit and warm cinnamon rolls.
