Simple Baked Pork Chops with Rice

INGREDIENTS

1 1/2 cups uncooked rice
1 can cream of chicken soup or mushroom soup
2 cups water
1/2 tsp. pepper
6 pork chops

DIRECTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Place rice in 13x9x2" baking dish.  Combine soup, water and pepper in a separate bowl and mix well.  Pour half the soup mixture over rice.  Place pork chops over rice and pour remaining soup mixture on top.  Cover and bake for 1 hour.
