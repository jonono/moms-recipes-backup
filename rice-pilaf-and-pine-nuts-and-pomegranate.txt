Rice Pilaf and Pine Nuts and Pomegranate

INGREDIENTS

1 1/2 cups basmati rice
2 Tbsp. olive oil
1/2 pine nuts
1 1/2 Tbsp. butter
1 onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1 bay leaf
1 1/2 cups chicken stock
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. dried thyme
pinch saffron
1/2 cup pomegranate seeds
2 Tbsp. chopped fresh dill
parsley or cilantro

DIRECTIONS

1. Rinse rice until water runs clear.  Cover with water and soak 30 to 60 minutes.  Drain in fine sieve, shaking as much water as possible.
2. In saucepan, heat oil over med heat. Fry pine nuts until golden.  With slotted spoon, remove nuts and set aside.  Add butter to pan with onion and garlic and fry stirring often until onion is tender.  Add rice and bay leaf.  Fry, stirring often but gently until rice is dry and thoroughly coated with fat, 4 to 5 minutes.  Add stock, 1 cup water, salt, thyme and saffron.  Bring to boil.  Now, reduce heat to low simmer and cover and cook (without uncovering) until liquid is absorbed, 20 to 25 minutes.  
3. With fork, fluff rice and stir in pine nuts.  Remove from heat, cover and let stand 5 to 10 minutes.  Transfer to serving plate.  Remove bay leaf and scatter  pomegranate seeds over rice.  Sprinkle with dill.
