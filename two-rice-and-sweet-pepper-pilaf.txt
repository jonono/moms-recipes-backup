Two Rice and Sweet Pepper Pilaf

about 6 servings

INGREDIENTS

1 Tbsp. butter
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 onion, chopped
1/2 tsp. dried thyme
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 1/2 cups chicken stock
1 Tbsp. white wine vinegar
1/2 cup wild rice, rinsed and drained
1 cup long grain rice
half sweet red pepper or yellow pepper, diced
1/2 tsp. salt

DIRECTIONS

1. Melt butter in large saucepan over med heat.  Cook garlic, onion, thyme and pepper, stirring occasionally until softened, about 5 minutes.  Add stock, 1 1/4 cups water and vinegar and bring to boil.  Reduce heat, cover, simmer 35 minutes.  Stir in long grain rice. Cover and simmer for another 30 minutes or until liquid is absorbed.  With fork, stir in pepper and salt.  Cover and let stand 5 minutes.
