Starbucks Pumpkin Bread

INGREDIENTS

1 1/2 cups flour
1 cup sugar
3/4 tsp. salt
1 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
1/4 tsp. cardamon
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. allspice
1/4 tsp. clove
2 eggs, beaten
1 cup pumpkin puree
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/2 tsp. vanilla
roasted and salted pumpkin seeds

DIRECTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 350.  In large bowl, add all the dry ingredients.  Sift dry ingredients together.  In smaller bowl, mix eggs, the oil and vanilla.  Pour over the dry ingredients and until lumps are out.  Do not over mix.  Pour into loaf pan sprayed with Pam.  Sprinkle pumpkin seeds over batter and bake 50-60 minutes until toothpick comes out clean.  Cool 5 minutes before turning out on cooling rack.  Allow to cool completely before cutting into it.
