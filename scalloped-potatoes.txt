Scalloped Potatoes

good basic recipe for 5-6 people

INGREDIENTS

1/4 cup butter
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup flour
1 tsp. chopped fresh thyme
3/4 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
2.5 cups milk
about 2 lb. potatoes 
1 small onion, sliced

DIRECTIONS

1. In saucepan, melt butter and cook garlic stirring 1 minutes.  Add flour, thyme, salt and pepper.  Cook another minute.  Gradually whisk in milk until boiling and thickened, about 8 minutes.
2. In 2L square baking dish, spray with Pam.  Layer thinly sliced potatoes (1/3), then onions and end with potatoes.  Pour sauce over top and ease sauce between layers with a knife.  Cayenne pepper on top and a bit more thyme.  
3. Cover, bake at 350 for 1 hour.  Uncover and bake another 30 minutes.  Let stand 5 minutes.
