Corn and Tomato Salad

INGREDIENTS

3 cups frozen kernel corn, thawed
2 Roma tomatoes, diced
2 Tbsp. chopped fresh cilantro
2 Tbsp. fresh lime juice
2 Tbsp. oil
1/4 tsp. sugar
1/4 tsp. salt
1 avocado, peeled and diced
fresh cilantro leaves

DIRECTIONS

1. Combine first 7 ingredients.  Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours and up to 24 hours.  Up to one hour before serving, stir in avocado and garnish with cilantro leaves.
