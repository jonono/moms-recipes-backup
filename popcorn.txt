Popcorn

INGREDIENTS

3 Tbsp. canola oil
1/3 cup popcorn kernels
1 covered heavy saucepan
butter and salt to taste

DIRECTIONS

1. Heat oil in pan with 4 kernels.  When they pop, remove pan from heat and add the rest of the kernels.  Count to 30 and replace pan on stove and shake until all popped.  Slide cover off a little to let steam escape.
