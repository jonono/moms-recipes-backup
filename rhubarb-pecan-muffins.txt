Rhubarb Pecan Muffins

INGREDIENTS

2 cups flour
3/4 cups sugar
1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
3/4 cup chopped pecans
1 large egg
1/4 cup oil
2 tsp. grated orange peel
3/4 cup orange juice
1 1/4 cups fresh rhubarb, finely chopped

DIRECTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  In a large bowl, combine dry ingredients.  In med. bowl, beat egg.  Stir in oil, orange peel and juice.  Add to flour all at once and stir just until batter is moist.  Stir in rhubarb.  
2. Bake 25 to 30 minutes.
