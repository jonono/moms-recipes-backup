Aromatic Beef Kabobs with Tzaziki

INGREDIENTS

1 lb lean ground beef
1/4 cup dry bread crumbs
2 Tbsp. minced fresh coriander
1 Tbsp. each minced ginger root and garlic
1/2 tsp. each cinnamon, pepper, dried oregano and thyme
1/4 tsp. each salt and cumin
4 pita beads (Greek style with no pockets)
2 cups lettuce shredded

DIRECTIONS

1. In bowl, blend first ingredients and divide into 12 portions.  Form into egg shapes and flatten slightly.  Thread onto skewers.  
2. Place on greased grill and close lid.  Grill turning twice, about 10 minutes.  Grill pita on both sides until crisp and serve with lettuce and meat.
