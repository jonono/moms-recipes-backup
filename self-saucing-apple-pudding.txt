Self Saucing Apple Pudding

INGREDIENTS

8 med apples
sugar, cinnamon and flour 
1 cup flour
3/4 cup sugar
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1 egg
2 Tbsp.  oil
3/4 cup milk
1 tsp. vanilla
2 1/4 cups boiling water
1 cup brown sugar

DIRECTIONS

1. Peel and core 8 medium apples.  Splice a layer in a greased 9x13" pan.  Sprinkle with sugar, cinnamon and flour.
2. Add wet ingredients to dry mixture and stir until moistened.  Drop by spoonfuls onto apples and spread to cover top.  
3. Mix 2 1/4 cups boiling water and 1 cup brown sugar.  Stir until sugar dissolves and pour over cake.  Bake 35-40 minutes at 350 degrees.
