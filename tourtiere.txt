Tourtiere

the best recipe I've ever found

INGREDIENTS

1 lb. ground pork and/or beef
1 small onion, finely chopped
1 small potato, grated
1 small garlic clove, crushed
1/4 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
1/4 tsp. celery salt
1/4 tsp. clove
1/8 tsp. cinnamon
1/8 tsp. nutmeg
1/2 cup water
pastry for double crust

DIRECTIONS

1. Cook and boil for 20 minutes.  Let cool in fridge overnight or a few hours.  Pour in crust and cover with top crust.  Bake at 400 for about 45 minutes.
2. Freezes well.
