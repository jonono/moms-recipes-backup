Candied Ginger Scones

INGREDIENTS

2 cups flour
1/3 cup sugar
1 Tbsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
1/2 cup candied ginger, chopped
1/2 cup frozen butter
3/4 cup cream and a bit more for brushing the scones

DIRECTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  In large bowl, mix dry ingredients with ginger.  Using large holes, grate the butter into it.  With fingertips. gently toss flour and butter.  In small bowl, mix egg and cream and pour into flour mixture and stir until combined.  Mixture should not be very wet and may require an extra tbsp. cream.  Turn dough and pat together into circle about 1" thick.  Cut the circles into 8 wedges and place on parchment lined baking sheet.  Brush lightly with cream and bake 12 to 15 minutes until golden.
