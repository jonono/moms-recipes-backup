Meatballs for Spaghetti Sauce

INGREDIENTS

1 1/2 lb. ground beef
1 cup fresh white bread crumbs (4 slices with crusts removed)
1/4 cup seasoned dried bread crumbs
2 Tbsp. chopped parsley
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. black pepper
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
1 extra large egg, beaten
3/4 cup warm water

DIRECTIONS

1. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl.  Gently form into 2" meatballs and place evenly on cookie sheet.  Cook at 375 for 30 minutes, turning halfway through.  Combine with sauce and serve!
