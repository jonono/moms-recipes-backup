Roasted Vegetables

Serves 12

INGREDIENTS

1 small butternut squash, cubed
2 red bell peppers, seeded and diced
1 sweet potato, peeled and cubed
3 Yukon Gold potatoes, cubed
1 red onion, quartered
1 Tbsp. chopped fresh thyme
2 Tbsp. chopped fresh rosemary
1/4 cup olive oil
2 Tbsp. balsamic vinegar
salt and pepper

DIRECTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 475 degrees.  In large bowl, combine your vegetables and separate red onion quarters into pieces.  In small bowl, combine herbs and oil and vinegar and toss with vegetables until well coated.  Spread evenly on large roasting pan.  Roast 35 to 40 minutes, stirring every 10 minutes.  
2. Note - Use fresh herbs
