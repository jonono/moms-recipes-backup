Maple Butter Tarts

INGREDIENTS

3/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup maple syrup
1/3 cup butter, melted
2 eggs
1 Tbsp. cider vinegar
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 cup chopped walnuts

DIRECTIONS

1. In bowl, whisk all together except for nuts.  Divide walnuts evenly between pastry shells.  Spoon scant 1/4 cup filling into each shell.  Bake at 350 for 20  to 25 minutes.

NOTES

Makes about a dozen
