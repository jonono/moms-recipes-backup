Banana Rum Bread Pudding

INGREDIENTS

4 eggs
1 cup milk
1 cup whipping cream
1/4 cup rum
1 cup brown sugar
1 Tbsp. vanilla extract
4 ripe bananas, mashed but still chunky
1 loaf your favourite bread, cubed
Whipping Cream
2 cups whipping cream
2 Tbsp. sugar
1 tsp. nutmeg, freshly grated
1 tsp. vanilla extract

DIRECTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Lightly oil a 13X9 baking dish.
2. Beat eggs in large bowl.  Add milk, cream, rum, brown sugar and vanilla.  Whisk until combined.  Stir in bananas.  Stir in bread cubes coating every piece.  Allow to rest until bread absorbs the custard, about 10 minutes.  Pour into baking dish and bake until set, cooked through and golden brown, about 40 minutes.
3. Whip cream with ingredients and serve with pudding.
