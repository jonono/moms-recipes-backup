Mom's Meatloaf

INGREDIENTS

1 tsp. prepared horseradish
1 tsp. dry mustard
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 pound ground beef
1/2 onion, finely chopped
1 egg
2 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 cup panko bread crumbs
1 squirt ketchup
2/3 cup beef broth

DIRECTIONS

1. In small bowl, mix horseradish, mustard, salt, ketchup and pepper and set aside.
2. In large bowl, mix meat with onion, egg, Worcestershire sauce and 1/3 cup beef broth.
3. Add contents of small bowl and mix thoroughly.
4. Add bread crumbs and combine well.
5. Place in a 1 pound loaf pan and pat into pan.
6. Pour 1/3 cup beef broth over mixture
7. 350 degrees for about an hour
