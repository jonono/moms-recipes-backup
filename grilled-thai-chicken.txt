Grilled Thai Chicken

INGREDIENTS

3 cloves garlic, minced
1 small onion, chopped
1 cup coconut milk
1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
1/4 cup soy sauce
2 Tbsp. chopped fresh ginger
1 tsp. pepper
1 tsp. Dijon mustard
1/2 tsp. curry powder
4 med. boneless chicken breasts
1/2 cup chopped green onions

DIRECTIONS

1. In blender, combine garlic through curry powder.  Reserve 1/4 of the marinade.  In a shallow baking dish, combine the breasts and marinade and sprinkle with green onions.  Refrigerate a few hours if possible.  Grill chicken basting with reserved marinade.
