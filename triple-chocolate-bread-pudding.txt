Triple Chocolate Bread Pudding

Makes 4-6 servings

INGREDIENTS

3 3/4 cups stale French bread
3 tbsp. butter, melted
1 1/2 cups milk
1 1/2 cups heavy whipping cream
3 squares (1 oz. each) bittersweet chocolate, chopped
3 eggs
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
3 tbsp. sugar
1/4 tsp. salt
1 1/2 cups white chocolate chips
3/4 cups semi sweet chocolate chips

DIRECTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Lightly spray a 9" square pan with vegetable pan spray.  Combine bread and melted butter and set aside.  Bring milk and cream to simmer.  Remove from heat and add bittersweet chocolate whisking until blended and smooth.  In another bowl, whisk eggs, vanilla, sugar and salt.  Stir in chocolate mixture and pour over bread.  Let sit 10 minutes.  Stir in white and semi sweet chocolate chips.   Pour mixture into pan. Bake 30-35 minutes or until firm to touch.  Cool 5 minutes and serve warm. Cut into squares and serve with berries and chocolate sauce if desired.

NOTES

Can serve with whipped cream
