Corn Marrakesh

INGREDIENTS

1/4 cup butter, softened
2 tsp. coriander
2 tsp. cumin
1 tsp. oregano
1/2 tsp. ginger
1/4 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
4 cobs corn, husked

DIRECTIONS

1. Combine all ingredients except corn.  Place each cob on a piece of foil.  Spread butter mixture evenly over corn.  Bring edges together and close up foil packets.  Cook over BBQ turning over often 15 to 20 minutes.
