Buttermilk Scones

INGREDIENTS

3 cups flour
1/3 cup sugar
1 tsp. salt
2 1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda
3/4 cup unsalted butter, frozen or very cold
1 cup buttermilk
1/2 cup currants (optional)
heavy cream for brushing

DIRECTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Combine dry ingredients in large bowl.  Add butter by grating it.  Mix with fingers to coarse meal.  Add buttermilk and mix just until combined.  Add currants.
2. Transfer dough to a floured board and divide into 2 parts.  Roll each into 3/4" discs.  Cut into 8 wedges and place on parchment line baking sheet.  Brush with cream and bake about 15 minutes or until lightly browned.  Serve warm.
