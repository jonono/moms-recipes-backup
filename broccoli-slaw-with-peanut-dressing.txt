Broccoli Slaw with Peanut Dressing

INGREDIENTS

1/4 natural peanut butter
2 Tbsp. lime juice
2 Tbsp. hot water
1 Tbsp. grated ginger
1 Tbsp. soya sauce
1 garlic clove, minced
2 tsp. lime zest
1/2 tsp. sugar
340g pkg. broccoli slaw mix
1/4 cup chopped toasted peanuts
2 green onions, thinly sliced

DIRECTIONS

1. Whisk first ingredients in a large bowl until combined.  Add slaw mix and stir well to coat.  Stir in peanuts and onions.
