Ragout de Boulette

a traditional French Canadian dish

INGREDIENTS

1 pound of pork/beef
1 onion
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. cloves
2 tsp. parsley
1 egg beaten
1/3 cup breadcrumbs
salt and pepper
1 tbsp oil
4 cups chicken stock
flour for thickening

DIRECTIONS

1. Blend first 8 ingredients and make into balls.  Fry in oil.  You may drain if you wish.
2. Add stock and let simmer 30 minutes.  Take balls out.  Thicken gravy with flour or Bisto and re add balls.
