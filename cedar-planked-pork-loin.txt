Cedar Planked Pork Loin

INGREDIENTS

1 untreated cedar plank (6x12")
2 tsp. paprika
1 tsp. oregano
1 tsp. sage
1 tsp. thyme
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. onion powder
1/2 tsp. ground pepper
1/4 tsp. cayenne
8 lb boneless pork loin roast, fat trimmed

DIRECTIONS

1. Soak your plank for 24 hours.  Combine the rub by mixing all the dry ingredients and rubbing it into your pork.  Let stand 30 minutes.
2. Rinse plank and place pork on it.  Place on BBQ and with lid down, cook over med heat ab out 1-1/2 hours or until thermometer reads 160 degrees.  If using a triple burner BBQ, turn center burner off for best results.  Transfer to platter and cover with foil.  Let stand 15 minutes before carving.  Serves about 12 people.
