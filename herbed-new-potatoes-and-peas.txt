Herbed New Potatoes and Peas

INGREDIENTS

1 1/2 lb. new potatoes, halved
1 cup frozen peas
1/4 cup Kraft Extra Virgin OIive Oil Pesto Parmesan Dressing
1/4 cup finely chopped fresh mint

DIRECTIONS

1. Cook potatoes until tender adding peas for the last 3 minutes.  Drain and return to pan.  Add remaining ingredients and toss until coated.
