Hot Fudge Pudding

INGREDIENTS

1 cup flour
2/3 cup sugar
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa
1/2 cup milk (whole best)
2 Tbsp. melted butter
1 tsp. vanilla
1 cup packed brown sugar
vanilla ice cream

DIRECTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 350.  Grease an 8x8 glass baking dish.  Combine flour, sugar, b. powder, salt, cinnamon and 1/4 cup cocoa.  Whisk in milk butter and vanilla just until smooth.  Spread into baking dish.  In small bowl, mix brown sugar, 1/4 cup cocoa and  sprinkle over batter.  Carefully, pour 1 1/2 cups boiling water over mixture.  Do not stir.  Bake 30 minutes.  Cool in pan on rack 10 minutes.
