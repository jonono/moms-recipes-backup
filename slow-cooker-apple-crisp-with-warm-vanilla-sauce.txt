Slow cooker apple crisp with warm vanilla sauce

INGREDIENTS

3 Tbsp. sugar
1 Tbsp. flour
1 tsp. ground cinnamon
8 Granny Smith apples, peeled and sliced
12 gingersnap cookies, finely crushed
3/4 cup quick cooking oats
1/2 cup chopped pecans
1/4 cup melted margarine
1 pkg. (4 serving) vanilla instant pudding
1/4 tsp. ground nutmeg
3 cups milk

DIRECTIONS

1. Mix sugar, flour and cinnamon in large bowl.  Add apple; toss to coat.  Place in slow cooker.
2. Combine cookie crumbs, oats, nuts and margarine; sprinkle with over apples.  Cover with lid and cook on high 2 to 3 hours or low 4 to 6 hours.
3. Beat pudding mix, nutmeg and milk in bowl with whisk 2 minutes.  Microwave on high 2 minutes, stirring after each minute.  Spoon over individual servings.  
4. Special addition - add 1/2 cup dried cranberries to the apple mixture before cooking.
