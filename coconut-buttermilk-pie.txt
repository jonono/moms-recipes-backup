Coconut Buttermilk Pie

INGREDIENTS

1/2 cup butter
1 1/2 cups sugar
4 eggs
2 Tbsp. flour
1/4 tsp. salt
1 cup shredded coconut
1 cup buttermilk
1 tsp. rum extract

DIRECTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 350.  Beat butter and sugar until creamy.  Add eggs beating well.  Add flour and salt beating at a low speed until blended.  Sir in coconut and buttermilk and rum extract.  Pour into uncooked pie shell.  Bake 1 hour until knife comes out clean.
