Spinach Salad with Special Raspberry Dressing

INGREDIENTS

3/4 cup mayo
3 Tbsp. raspberry vinegar
2 Tbsp. sugar
1 Tbsp. fresh lemon juice
1 tsp. dry mustard
1/4 tsp. each salt and white pepper
3/4 cup cooked crumbled bacon
2 cups sliced fresh mushrooms
8 cups torn spinach

DIRECTIONS

1. Whisk first 7 ingredients (Mayo through pepper).  Combine bacon, mushrooms and spinach and toss with enough dressing to coat.  Serves 6 to 8
