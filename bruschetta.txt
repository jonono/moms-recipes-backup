Bruschetta

INGREDIENTS

1/4 cup finely chopped onion
2 Tbsp. olive oil
3 large ripe tomatoes
1 pkg (1 oz) fresh basil
3 cloves garlic, crushed
1 Tbsp. balsamic vinegar
salt and pepper

DIRECTIONS

1. Saute onions in oil until transparent.   Remove from heat.
2. Chop tomatoes and place in bowl along with chopped basil, cooked onions and oil, garlic, vinegar and salt and pepper to taste.
3. Refrigerate several hours.
