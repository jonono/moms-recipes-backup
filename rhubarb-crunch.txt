Rhubarb Crunch

INGREDIENTS

1 cup flour
3/4 cup quick cooking oatmeal
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup melted butter
1 tsp. cinnamon
4 cups diced rhubarb
1 cup sugar
1 Tbsp. cornstarch
1 cup water
1 tsp. vanilla

DIRECTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 350.  Mix flour, oatmeal, brown sugar, melted butter and cinnamon in a bowl.  Press half into a 9" square pan and cover with rhubarb.
2. In saucepan, combine sugar and cornstarch.  Add water and vanilla and stir to mix.  Cook until thick and clear.  Carefully pour over rhubarb.  Sprinkle evenly with remaining crumb mixture and bake 1 hour.
