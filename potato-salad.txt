Potato Salad

INGREDIENTS

2 lbs. boiled baby red potatoes, cut in half (boil about 12 minutes, but check for tenderness with a knife before draining)
1 tsp. cumin
1 tsp. paprika
1/2 tsp. cayenne
1/2 tsp. salt
freshly ground pepper
1/4 to 1/2 cup mayonnaise.  Do not use light mayo or Miracle Whip.
1/2 cup slightly drained salsa.  Use fresh salsa (Ziggy's from Superstore).
palmful of chopped cilantro

DIRECTIONS

NOTES

quite spicy so perhaps cut back on the cayenne pepper a bit
