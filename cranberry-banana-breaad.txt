Cranberry Banana Breaad

good for 2 loaves 
Can use 2 cups cranberries
Bake less than an hour

INGREDIENTS

1/2 cup softened butter
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 cup mashed banana
1 tsp. each vanilla and lemon juice
2 cups flour
1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. each of soda and salt
1/4 cup milk
1 1/2 cups chopped cranberries
1/2 cup chopped pecans

DIRECTIONS

1. Beat butter and sugar until fluffy.  Beat in eggs one at a time.  Beat in bananas, vanilla and lemon juice.  In separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt.  Stir into butter mixture alternately with milk.  Fold in cranberries and pecans.  Bake at 350 about an hour.
