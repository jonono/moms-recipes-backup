Land of Nod Cinammon Buns

Perfect for Xmas morning or when having company

INGREDIENTS

20 unbaked frozen dinner rolls
1 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup instant vanilla pudding mix
2 tbsp. ground cinnamon
raisins and or nuts
1/3 cup butter, melted

DIRECTIONS

1. Grease a Bundt pan and add frozen rolls.  Sprinkle with sugar, pudding, cinnamon, raisins and nuts.  Pour melted butter all over.  Cover with a clean damp cloth and leave out at room temperature overnight.  
2. In the morning, preheat to 350 degrees and bake 25 minutes.  Let sit 5 minutes before turning out.
