Banana Split Cake

INGREDIENTS

1 1/2 cups graham crumbs
1/3 cup butter, melted
2 pkg. cream cheese, softened
1 cup sugar
1 can crushed pineapple (14 oz), drained
6 med. bananas, divided
2 cups cold milk
2 pkg (4 serving size) vanilla instant pudding
2 cups thawed cool whip
1 cup chopped pecans

DIRECTIONS

1. Mix crumbs and butter and  press onto bottom of foil lined 13x9 pan.  Freeze 10 minutes. 
2. Beat cream cheese and sugar with electric mixer until well blended.  Carefully spread cream cheese over crust and top with pineapple.  Slice 4 of the bananas and arrange over pineapple.
3. Pour milk into a large bowl.  Add dry pudding  mixes and beat with wire whisk 2 minutes or until well blended.  Stir in 1 cup cool whip and spread over banana layer in pan.  Top with remaining cool whip and sprinkle with pecans.  Refrigerate 5 hours.  Just before serving, slice remaining bananas and arrange over dessert.
