Leftover Hambone Soup

a good hearty soup

INGREDIENTS

1 leftover hambone
1 tbsp. olive oil
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 onion, diced
2 carrots, peeled and diced
1 potato, peeled and diced
1 cup white kidney beans, drained and rinsed
3/4 cup frozen corn kernels
3/4 tsp. fresh thyme leaves
2 bay leaves
salt and pepper to taste
1 -1/2 cups leftover diced ham

DIRECTIONS

1. Place hambone in pot and add enough water to cover the bone halfway, about 6-7 cups.  Bring to boil and reduce heat to simmer until very fragrant, about 30 minutes to an hour.  Remove and discard bone.
2. Heat oil and add garlic, onion, carrots and potato.  Cook, stirring occasionally until onions are translucent, about 2-3 minutes.  Stir in ham stock, beans, corn, thyme and bay leaves.  Season with salt and pepper.
3. Bring to boil, reduce heat and simmer until potatoes are cooked, about 12 minutes.  Add ham.
