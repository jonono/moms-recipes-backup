Rhubarb Strawberry Crisp

Source: Dessert Crisp rhubarb strawberry

INGREDIENTS

4 cups rhubarb pieces (about 1 lb)
2 cup sliced strawberries
1 cup sugar
1 1/3 cup flour, divided
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup old fashioned oats
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
1/2 cup butter, melted and cooled

DIRECTIONS

1. Combine rhubarb, strawberries, sugar, 1/3 cup flour and cinnamon and transfer to greased 9x13 baking dish.
2. In another bowl, combine remaining 1 cup flour with brown sugar, oats and nutmeg.  Add butter and blend well to create the streusel.  Sprinkle over fruit and bake at 350 for 35-40 minutes.
