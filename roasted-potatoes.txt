Roasted Potatoes

Takes about 1.5 hours total time

INGREDIENTS

8 large potatoes, quartered
1 red pepper, cubed into 1" pieces
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup butter
salt and pepper to taste
1 tsp. dried oregano
1/2 lemon, juice only
1 cup water
1 lemon

DIRECTIONS

1. In a large heavy roasting pan, add 1 cubed red pepper to potatoes, olive oil, butter, salt and pepper, oregano, 1/2 lemon juice, water and a bit of cayenne pepper too.  Roast at 400 for 30 minutes.
2. Toss potatoes and peppers and return to oven for another 20 minutes.
3. Toss again for another 15 minutes.
4. Toss again, turn off heat and leave in oven another 15 minutes.  
5. Squeeze lemon juice onto potatoes, let sit a few minutes and serve.
