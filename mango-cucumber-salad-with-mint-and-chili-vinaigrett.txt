Mango Cucumber Salad with Mint and Chili Vinaigrett

INGREDIENTS

1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped
1 tbsp. fresh ginger, peeled and grated
1/2 tsp. garlic, crushed
1/2 orange, juice and zest
1/2 lime, juice and zest
3 tbsp. sweet chili sauce
1/4 rice wine vinegar
1 tsp. sesame oil
1/4 cup vegetable oil

DIRECTIONS

1. Salad
2. arugula or spicy greens
3. fresh mangoes, peeled and thinly sliced
4. red pepper, julienne sliced
5. orange pepper, julienne sliced
6. yellow pepper, julienne sliced
7. red onion, thinly sliced
8. long English cucumber, thinly sliced on the diagonal
9. slivered almonds
10. Arrange greens first on plate followed by ingredients and drizzle with vinaigrette.  You can also. use a large salad bowl
