Cinnamon Toast Strata

`6 servings

INGREDIENTS

1/4 cup butter, softened
1 Tbsp. cinnamon
12 slices plain or raisin bread
1/4 cup sugar (divided)
6 eggs
1 1/2 cups milk
3 Tbsp. maple syrup
icing sugar

DIRECTIONS

1. Mash butter with cinnamon and spread on bread.  Arrange 5 or 6 slices in bottom of 9x13" pan, buttered side down, trimming to fit.  Sprinkle with 2 Tbsp. sugar.  Cut remaining slices in half and arrange over top, buttered side down.  Sprinkle with remaining sugar.  Beat eggs, milk and maple syrup, blending well in a bowl.  Pour slowly over bread and cover with foil.  Refrigerate overnight.  Bake in 350 oven uncovered 35 to 40 minutes or until puffed, golden and set.  Sprinkle with icing sugar.
