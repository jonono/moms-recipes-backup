Sweet and Sour Watermelon and Cucumber Salad

INGREDIENTS

1/2 cup cider vinegar
1 cup water
1/2 cup sugar
1 Tbsp. poppy seeds
1 bunch green onions, trimmed and chopped
3 cups seedless watermelon balls or small chunks
2 English cucumbers, peeled, seeded and sliced

DIRECTIONS

1. Mix vinegar, water, sugar and poppy seeds in a bowl.  Stir in green onions.  Place watermelon and cucumber in a serving bowl and pour dressing over top.
