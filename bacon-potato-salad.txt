Bacon Potato Salad

INGREDIENTS

2 lb small red potatoes, quartered
3/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper
1/4 tsp. black pepper
2 Tbsp. freshly squeezed lemon juice
1/3 cup finely chopped celery
2 Tbsp. finely chopped green onions
1 Tbsp. chopped fresh parsley
4 hard boiled eggs, chopped
3/4 cup mayo
1 1/2 tsp. whole grain mustard
4 strips bacon, crumbled

DIRECTIONS

1. Cook potatoes until fork tender, about 10 minutes.  Drain and transfer to bowl.  While warm, season with salt, cayenne, black pepper and lemon juice.  Toss well and add celery, green onions, parsley and eggs.  
2. In small bowl, stir the mayo and mustard together.  Add to salad.  Sprinkle with bacon and toss gently.
