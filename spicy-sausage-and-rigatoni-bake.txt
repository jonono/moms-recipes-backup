Spicy Sausage and Rigatoni Bake

Great bake pasta feeds at least 8 people.

INGREDIENTS

1 Tbsp. olive oil
2 onions, sliced
1 red pepper, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 tsp. fennel seeds, crushed
1 lb. hot Italian sausage
2 jars (700ml each) tomato-basil pasta sauce
1 Tbsp. wine vinegar
12 oz. rigatoni pasta (about 5 cups, 1.25L)
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 Tbsp. chopped fresh parsley

DIRECTIONS

1. In Dutch oven, heat oil and cook onions for about 5 minutes.  Add red pepper, garlic, fennel seeds and cook over med heat for 10 minutes or until onions are very soft and golden.  Transfer to bowl.  Add sliced sausage to skillet and cook until browned.  Drain off fat.  Stir in pasta sauce and vinegar.  Return vegetable mixture to pan and simmer for 15 minutes.  
2. Meanwhile, in large pot of boiling water, cook pasta until tender but firm - about 8 minutes.  Drain but reserve 1 cup cooking water.  Return pasta to pot.  Add sauce and reserved water.  
3. Combine cheeses and set aside 1 cup.  Stir remaining cheese into pasta mixture.   Pour into large baking dish pressing pasta to submerge.  Sprinkle with reserved cheese and parsley.  Can make ahead and refrigerate.  Cover and bake at 400 degrees for 30 to 45 minutes.  Uncover and bake another 15 minutes or until golden.
