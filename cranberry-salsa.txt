Cranberry Salsa

INGREDIENTS

1 tsp. oil
1/2 cup chopped red onion
2 cups fresh cranberries
3 Tbsp. sugar
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 Tbsp. finely grated lime peel
1 Tbsp. lime juice
1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
2 tsp. minced jalapeno peppers
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 cup finely chopped cilantro

DIRECTIONS

1. Heat oil.  Add onion and cook 2 minutes.  Stir in cranberries, sugar and garlic.  Bring to boil and reduce to simmer for 5 minutes.  Stir in lime peel, juice, W. sauce, jalapeno and salt into cranberries.  Remove from heat and add cilantro.
