Jamaican Rum Cake

INGREDIENTS

1/2 cup chopped pecans
1 box yellow cake mix
1 small box instant vanilla pudding
4 eggs
1/2 cup oil
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup dark rum
Glaze
1/2 cup butter
1 cup sugar
1/4 cup water
1/4 cup rum

DIRECTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees.  Spray a Bundt pan with Pam and sprinkle with pecans in bottom.
2. Place cake mix, pudding, eggs, oil, water and rum in large bowl.  Beat with mixer for 2 minutes.  Pour in prepared pan and bake 1 hour.  
3. While cake is baking, combine all ingredients for glaze except rum in saucepan.  Bring to boil 1 minute.  Remove from heat and add rum.  When cake is done, pierce thoroughly with toothpick and immediately pour glaze over top.  Cool completely before removing from pan.
