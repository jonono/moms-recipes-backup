Chocolate Banana Cake with Chocolate Ganache and Caramel Drizzle

INGREDIENTS

2 cups mashed ripe bananas
2 tsp. lemon juice
2 1/2 cups flour
1/2 cup cocoa
1 1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. salt
1/2 cup oil
2 cups brown sugar, packed
3 eggs
1 Tbsp. vanilla
1 1/2 cups buttermilk
1/2 cup choc chips
Ganache
1 cup choc chips
6 Tbsp. milk
1/2 cup caramel sauce

DIRECTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  Grease and dust Bundt pan with extra cocoa powder.
2. In bowl, mix banana with lemon juice and set aside.
3. In another bowl, mix dry ingredients.
4. In Kitchenaid mixer, beat oil and sugar.  Beat in eggs one at a time and add vanilla.  Blend in flour and buttermilk in 2 additions.  Fold in bananas and choc chips.  Bake 55 minutes.  Cool in pan 30 minutes, then turn out on cake plate.  
5. To make ganache, place choc chips and milk in bowl and cook on high 1 minute.  Whisk until smooth and pour over cake.  Drizzle also with caramel sauce.
