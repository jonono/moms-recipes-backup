Barbecued Pork Tenderloin

INGREDIENTS

2 pork tenderloins
2 tsp. cornstarch
1 green onion, finely chopped
2 Tbps. finely chopped fresh coriander
Marinade:
1/2 cup orange juice
1/4 cup tomato paste
2 Tbsp. grainy mustard
3 cloves garlic, minced
4 tsp. chili powder
1 1/2 tsp. sugar
1 tsp. ground cumin and coriander
1/2 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/4 tsp. salt

DIRECTIONS

1. In bowl, whisk the marinade ingredients.  Add pork and turn to coat.  Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours and up to 24 hours.  Remove pork from marinade and set aside marinade in a small saucepan.  Grill pork on greased grill with lid closed on medium heat, about 20 minutes turning 3 times.  Meanwhile, bring reserved marinade and 3/4 cup water to boil.  Simmer and reduce to 1 cup, about 7 minutes.  In small bowl, whisk cornstarch with 1 Tbsp. water and whisk into marinade until thickened and glossy.  Keep warm.  Transfer pork to cutting board and tent with foil about 10 minutes before slicing across the grain.  Sprinkle with onion and coriander and serve sauce on the side.
