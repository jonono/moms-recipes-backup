Baked Brie with Maple Syrup

Super easy and always delicious

INGREDIENTS

1 round Brie cheese
chopped walnuts
real maple syrup
baguette slices

DIRECTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees.  Place the cheese into baking dish.  Sprinkle with nuts and pour syrup over.  Bake about 10 minutes until warm and soft.  Serve with baguette slices.
