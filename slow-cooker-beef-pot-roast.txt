Slow Cooker Beef Pot Roast

INGREDIENTS

1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
1 tsp. onion powder
1 tsp. garlic powder
3 lb. boneless blade roast
1 carton (900mL) Beef broth, less sodium
1/2 cup cider vinegar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 small onion, coarsely chopped
2 med carrots, coarsely chopped
2 stalks celery, coarsely chopped
1/4 cup flour
1/4 cup water

DIRECTIONS

1. In a small bowl, combine salt, pepper, onion and garlic powders and sprinkle over meat.  In slow cooker, combine broth, vinegar and sugar and mix well.  Add the beef and vegetables.  Cover and cook on LOW about 8 hours.
2. Half an hour before serving, remove beef and vegetables to a plate and tent with foil.  Skim fat from broth and turn heat to high.  Mix flour and water and add to broth.  Replace lid and cook for 30 minutes.
