Super Summer Whole Grain Salad

INGREDIENTS

1 cup precooked five grain blend (such as PC)
1 carrot, diced
1 yellow pepper, diced
1 small zucchini, diced
3 radishes, halved and thinly sliced
3/4 cup cooked peas
4 cups lightly packed baby arugula
1/3 cup pumpkin seeds, toasted
Chive-Citrus Vinaigrette
3 Tbsp. orange juice
2 Tbsp. olive oil
1 Tbsp. white wine vinegar
1/4 tsp. each salt and pepper
1/4 cup chopped fresh chives

DIRECTIONS

1. Cook grain blend in 4 cups boiling water about 10 to 12 minutes, uncovered.  Drain and transfer to large bowl.  Let cool.
2. Add vegetables.
3. Meanwhile, whisk together vinaigrette ingredients.  Toss with salad and sprinkle with pumpkin seeds.
