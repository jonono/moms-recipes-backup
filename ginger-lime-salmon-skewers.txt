Ginger Lime Salmon Skewers

INGREDIENTS

4 skinless centre-cut salmon fillets (about 6oz each)
1" piece ginger root, minced
1 green pepper, seeded and minced
3 Tbsp. chopped cilantro
2 Tbsp. soya sauce
1 Tbsp. lime juice
2 tsp. oil
pinch sugar
4 green onions, cut into 1 1/2" pieces
1 sweet red pepper, cut into 1 1/2" pieces
4 lime wedges

DIRECTIONS

1. Cut salmon into  1 1/2" cubes to make 24 pieces.  In large glass bowl, mix ginger, pepper, cilantro, soy sauce, lime juice, oil and sugar.  Add salmon and toss to coat.  Cover and refrigerate up to 30 minutes.
2. Beginning and ending with green onions, thread salmon, red pepper and green onions loosely onto metal skewers, reserving marinade.
3. Place on greased grill over medium heat and brush with marinade.  Close lid and grill, turning twice until fish flakes, about 10 minutes.  Serve with lime wedges.
