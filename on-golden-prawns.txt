On Golden Prawns

INGREDIENTS

1 tsp. olive oil
1/2 cup each minced onion and red pepper
1 tsp. minced garlic
1 tsp. cumin
3/4 tsp. coriander
1/2 tsp. curry powder
1 cup light coconut milk
1 tsp. sugar
1/4 tsp. crushed red pepper flakes
1 lb. uncooked jumbo shrimp, peeled and deveined
1 Tbsp. cornstarch
2 Tbsp. chopped fresh cilantro
hot cooked rice

DIRECTIONS

1. Cook onions, red pepper and garlic in large nonstick pot over med heat, about 3 minutes.  Add cumin, coriander and curry powder.  Cook one more minute.  Add coconut milk, sugar and crushed red pepper flakes.  Bring to boil and then reduce heat and simmer uncovered 2 minutes.  Stir in shrimp and a handful of frozen green peas (if you wish).  Increase heat to high and cook stirring about 4 minutes.  
2. In small bowl, combine cornstarch with 1 Tbsp. water and add to shrimp mixture.  Cook until bubbly and thickened about 1 minute.  Stir in cilantro and remove from heat.  Serve over hot rice.
