Steak Kabobs

INGREDIENTS

1/3 cup red wine
1/2 cup oil
2 to 3 cloves garlic, crushed
1/2 tsp. marjoram
1/2 tsp. rosemary
1/2 tsp. basil
1/2 tsp. oregano
1 1/2 tsp. salt
2 Tbsp. ketchup
1 Tbsp. vinegar
1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
3 lbs. sirloin steak, cut into 2" cubes
2 cups large mushrooms
green pepper, onion, cherry tomatoes

DIRECTIONS

1. In large glass bowl, combine all ingredients and add meat and mushrooms.  Cover tightly and let marinade overnight in fridge.
