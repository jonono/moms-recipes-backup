Claire's Banana Slice

INGREDIENTS

2 cups graham crumbs
1/2 cup melted butter
2 cups icing sugar
2/3 cup soft butter
2 eggs, well beaten
28 oz can drained pineapple
3 bananas sliced
2 cups whipping cream
slivered almonds

DIRECTIONS

1. Mix graham crumbs and butter and press into 9x13 pan.  Mix icing sugar, butter and eggs and spread on crumb base.  Spread pineapple over icing sugar, then bananas.  Spread whipped cream over banana layer.  Chill 8 hours.
