Heirloom Tomato Salad

INGREDIENTS

2 tbsp. EVOO
1 tbsp. sherry vinegar
pinch of salt and pepper
450g mini heirloom tomatoes, halved or quartered
1 rib celery (including leaves), thinly sliced
1/3 cup chopped fresh parsley

DIRECTIONS

1. Whisk together first 3 ingredients.  Add the rest and toss to coat.
