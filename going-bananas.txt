Going Bananas

INGREDIENTS

1 1/2 cups sugar
3 eggs
3/4 cup oil
2 cups flour
2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. allspice
2 cups mashed bananas
1/2 cup raisins

DIRECTIONS

1. Set oven to 350 degrees.  Grease and flour a Bundt pan.  Beat sugar and eggs, until creamy.  Mix in oil.  Sift flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon and allspice.  Add to creamed mixture.  Mix in bananas and raisins.   Pour into pan and bake about 1 hour.  Very moist cake.
