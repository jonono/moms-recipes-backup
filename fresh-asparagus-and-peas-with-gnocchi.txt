Fresh Asparagus and Peas with Gnocchi

Yield: about 4 servings

INGREDIENTS

about 5-6 strips of bacon  (use a smoky chipotle chili bacon if you can find it)
1 lb. asparagus (trimmed and cut into pieces)
1/2 cup chopped shallots
3 cloves garlic, minced 
salt and pepper
1 lb. (454g) potato gnocchi (fresh, frozen or shelf stable)
1/2 cup peas, fresh or frozen
grated Parmesan cheese

DIRECTIONS

1. In a large skillet, cook bacon until crisp and set aside on paper toweled lined plate.  Now, add asparagus, shallots, garlic, salt and pepper and cook stirring often about 7 minutes.  Meanwhile, cook gnocchi in a pot of large boiling water.  Add peas in last minute of cooking.  Gnocchi is cooked when it floats to the top.  Drain, reserving 1/2 cup of the cooking water.  Crumble bacon and add to skillet along with reserved cooking water, gnocchi and Parmesan cheese.
