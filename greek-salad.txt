Greek Salad

INGREDIENTS

1/2 cup olive oil
3 Tbsp. red wine vinegar
1 tsp. dried oregano
1/2 tsp. salt
1 head romaine lettuce, torn into pieces
1/3 cup chopped green or white onion
2 radishes, thinly sliced
1 green pepper, cut into strips
1/2 thinly sliced cucumber
1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
1/2 cup black olives

DIRECTIONS

1. In a small bowl, whisk oil, vinegar, oregano and salt together.  Dressing can be made ahead kept refrigerated several days.
