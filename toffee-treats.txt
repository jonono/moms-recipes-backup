Toffee Treats

a family favourite every Xmas

INGREDIENTS

Crust
1 cup flour
1 cup oats
1 cup packed brown sugar
1 tsp. soda
1/2 cup butter
Combine and pat firmly into pam sprayed 9x13" pan.  Bake at 350 for 10 minutes
Topping
1/2 cup corn syrup
1/3 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup whipping cream
1 1/2 cups sliced almonds
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla

DIRECTIONS

1. Combine first 4 ingredients in med. saucepan and over over med heat until it boils, stirring.  Remove from heat and stir in almonds and vanilla.  Pour over crust and bake at 350 for 15 to 20 minutes.  Cool on wire rack and cut into triangles.  Store in fridge.
