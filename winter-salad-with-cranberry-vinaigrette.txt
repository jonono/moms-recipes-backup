Winter Salad with Cranberry Vinaigrette

10 to 12

INGREDIENTS

1/4 cup cranberry juice
1/4 cup rice vinegar
1 Tbsp. honey
1 tsp. Dijon mustard
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 cup oil
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
2 Tbsp. finely chopped green onions
8 cups mixed baby greens
2 cups shredded red cabbage
1/2 cup thinly sliced red pepper
1/3 cup thinly sliced red onion
1/3 cup dried cranberries
1/3 cup shelled pumpkin seeds

DIRECTIONS

1. Whisk juice, vinegar, honey, mustard and salt until combined.  Gradually whisk in oil.  Stir in cilantro and green onion.
2. Combine remaining ingredients in salad bowl.  Add vinaigrette and toss.
