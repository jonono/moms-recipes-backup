Soft and Chewy Molasses Cookies

Makes 3 dozen,

INGREDIENTS

5 cups flour
2 Tbsp. ginger
4 tsp. baking soda
2 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp.  nutmeg
1/4 tsp. salt
1 1/2 cups softened butter
1/2 cup sugar
2 cups fancy molasses
1 egg
1 tsp. vanilla
golden granulated sugar crystals

DIRECTIONS

1. Combine first 6 ingredients in a bowl (flour through salt) and set aside.  Beat butter and sugar until fluffy using med speed.  Add molasses and beat until caramel coloured.  Beat in egg and vanilla.
2. Using low speed, gradually in flour mixture just until blended.  Chill dough in freezer for 1 hour.  Shape 2 Tbsp. dough into balls, roll into sugar crystals  and place 2" apart on parchment paper-lined cookie sheets.  Bake at 375 for 15 to 17 minutes until tops of cookies are cracked.
