Spiced Pumpkin Muffins

INGREDIENTS

2 cups flour
2/3 cup brown sugar
1/3 cup white sugar
1 Tbsp. baking powder
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. ground ginger
1/2 cup butter, melted
1/2 cup pure pumpkin
2 large eggs, lightly beaten
1/3 cup buttermilk

DIRECTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Combine dry ingredients in large bowl.  Combine butter, pumpkin, eggs and buttermilk in med bowl.  Add to flour mixture and stir just until moistened.  Spoon into muffin cups and bake for 15 to 20 minutes.  Cool 5 minutes.
