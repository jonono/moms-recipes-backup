Cheesy Greek Pinwheel Appetizers

INGREDIENTS

1 tub (250 g) Philadelphia Herb and Garlic cream cheese spread
1 cup Kraft Feta with Oregano, Sun Dried Tomatoes and Cracker Peppercorns Cheese
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1 pkg. (300g) frozen chopped spinach, thawed, well drained
2 green onions, finely choppped
6 large flour tortillas

DIRECTIONS

1. Mix all ingredients except tortillas until blended.  Spread on tortillas and roll up.  Wrap individually in plastic wrap and refrigerate 2 hours.
2. Heat oven to 375 degrees.  Unwrap tortillas, trim and discard ends.  Cut each roll-up diagonally into 10 slices.  Place on parchment lined pan and bake 10 minutes, turning after 5 minutes.  
3. Roll- ups can be made and refrigerated up to 24 hours before slicing and baking.  
4. Serve with tzatziki.
