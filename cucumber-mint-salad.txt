Cucumber Mint Salad

INGREDIENTS

3 garlic cloves
1 English cucumber
1/4 small red onion
1/2 cup coarsely chopped fresh mint
1/2 cup sour cream
2 Tbsp. olive oil
1/4 tsp. salt
pinches of black pepper
1 head Boston Lettuce

DIRECTIONS

1. Mince garlic. Slice cucumber in half lengthwise.  Using a small spoon, scrape out and discard seeds.  Thinly slice cucumber.  Finely chop onion and mint.  Place garlic, cucumber, onion and mint in a large bowl.  In a small bowl, stir sour cream with oil, salt and pepper until blended, then stir into cucumber mixture.  
2. Salad keeps for about a half day in fridge.  Spoon salad on lettuce leaves on serving plate.
