Spicy Garlic Cumin Flank Steak

INGREDIENTS

2 cloves garlic, grated
2 tsp. packed brown sugar
2 tsp. ground cumin
1 tsp. chipotle chili powder
450 g beef marinating flank steak

DIRECTIONS

1. In bowl, stir together 1 tbsp. vegetable oil with garlic, brown sugar, cumin, chili powder, 1/4 tsp. pepper.  Rub all over steak and let marinade up to 8 hours ahead.
2. Add 1/2 tsp. salt to meat before placing on greased grill over med-high heat.  Close lid and grill, turning once until med rare, about 8 minutes. Let stand 5 minutes before slicing against the grain.
