Green Chile Roll ups

INGREDIENTS

1/2 cup (1/2 of 250g tub) Philadelphia Light Cream Cheese Spread
2 Tbsp. chopped green chilies
1/4 tsp. cumin
4 small whole wheat tortillas
1/2 cup finely chopped red peppers
1/2 cup Tex Mex shredded cheese

DIRECTIONS

1. Mix cream cheese, chiles and cumin.  Spread onto tortillas.  Top with remaining ingredients.  Roll up tortillas tightly and cut each crosswise into 5 slices.  You can prepare ahead up to 4 hours but do not cut into slices until just before serving.
