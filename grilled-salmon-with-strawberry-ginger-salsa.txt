Grilled Salmon with Strawberry Ginger Salsa

INGREDIENTS

1 1/2 cups strawberries hulled and coarsely chopped
2 Tbsp. chopped red onion
2 Tbsp. fresh lime juice
1 Tbsp. minced fresh ginger
1/2 Tbsp. honey
1 Tbsp. chopped fresh basil
1 Tbsp. chopped cilantro
4 salmon fillets (about 8oz) each
1 tsp. olive oil
salt & pepper

DIRECTIONS

1. Mix strawberries, onion, lime juice, ginger and honey in a small bowl.   Set aside a while for flavours to combine.
2. Brush salmon with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.  Grill on BBQ over med-high heat for 2 to 3 minutes per side.  
3.  Add basil and cilantro to strawberry mixture and spoon over salmon for serving.
