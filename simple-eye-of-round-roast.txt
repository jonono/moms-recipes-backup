Simple Eye of Round Roast

INGREDIENTS

4 lb eye of round beef roast
olive oil, as needed
kosher or sea salt and pepper, to taste
2 tbsp. grainy mustard
4 sprigs fresh rosemary
4 cloves garlic, peeled and smashed

DIRECTIONS

1. Liberally coat beef with olive oil, salt and pepper.  Wrap in plastic and refrigerate overnight.  One hour before cooking, take roast out of fridge, unwrap and coat it with grainy mustard.  Place the rosemary sprigs and garlic in a roasting pan, then top with the beef.  Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Place pan in oven and roast for 30 minutes, then baste with any drippings.  Reduce heat to 300 degrees and cook to an internal temperature of 120 for med rare, about 60 to 75 minutes, basting every 30 minutes.  Remove from oven and let rest 20 minutes before carving.
