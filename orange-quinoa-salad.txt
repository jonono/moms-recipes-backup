Orange Quinoa Salad

INGREDIENTS

1/4 cup olive oil
1 tbsp. lemon juice
1 tbsp. white wine vinegar
pinch salt and pepper
3 cups cooked cooled quinoa
3 cups lightly packed baby arugula
2 oranges, peeled and segmented
1 green onion, thinly sliced

DIRECTIONS

1. In large bowl, stir first 4 ingredients and then add the rest.
