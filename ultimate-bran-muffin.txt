Ultimate Bran Muffin

the best I've ever found

INGREDIENTS

1.5 cups natural bran
3/4 cup flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. baking powder
1 egg
3/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup oil
1 cup sour milk (add 4 tbsp. vinegar to milk)
2 tbsp. molasses
1 cup raisins

DIRECTIONS

1. Combine first 5 ingredients in bowl.  In smaller bowl, beat egg, brown sugar, oil, milk and molasses.  Add all at once to dry mixture.  Stir until moistened with raisins.  Bake 375 for about 20 minutes.

NOTES

Makes a dozen
