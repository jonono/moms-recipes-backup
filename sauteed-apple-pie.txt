Sauteed Apple Pie

INGREDIENTS

2 Tbsp. butter
2 Tbsp. canola oil
4-5 lbs. apples (8-10) peeled, cored and sliced
2 Tbsp. lemon juice
1/2 cup sugar
3 Tbsp. flour
1 tsp. cinnamon
pinch of salt
pastry for double crust pie

DIRECTIONS

1. In large skillet, heat the butter and  oil over med high  heat.  Add apples and saute turning occasionally for 5 to 6 minutes until starting to soften and turn golden.  Sprinkle with lemon juice, sugars, flour, cinnamon and salt and cook for another 3 minutes until thick and golden.  Set aside to cool slightly.
2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Fill pastry with apple mixture.  Brush the top with cream and sprinkle with sugar.  Bake for about 40 minutes.
