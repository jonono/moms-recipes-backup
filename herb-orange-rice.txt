Herb Orange Rice

INGREDIENTS

1/4 cup butter
2/3 cup chopped celery (with leaves)
2 Tbsp. minced onion
1 1/2 cup water
1 Tbsp. grated orange peel
1 cup orange juice
1 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. thyme leaves
1 cup rice (white long grain)

DIRECTIONS

1. Cook celery and onion in hot butter.  Add water, orange peel, juice, salt and thyme.  Heat to boiling and stir in rice.  Reduce to low.  Cover and simmer 15 to 20 minutes.
