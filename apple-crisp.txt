Apple Crisp

INGREDIENTS

4 to 5 med cooking apples, peeled, cored and sliced
3 to 4 Tbsp. sugar
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
dash salt
Topping
3/4 cup flour
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
1/3 cup butter
1/4 cup oats
1/4 cup chopped walnuts

DIRECTIONS

1. Mix apples with sugar, cinnamon and a touch of salt.  Place in 8" square pan sprayed with Pam.  For the topping, mix flour, sugar, cinnamon, oats and walnuts.  Cut in butter until coarse crumbs and sprinkle over apples.
2. Preheat oven to 375 and bake for 35 to 45 minutes until apples are tender and crumbs are golden.  Serve with ice cream.
